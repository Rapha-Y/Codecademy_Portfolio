Front-end Developer & UX Designer

I'm a fresh developer, with an affection for web design.

Raphael Yuji

My first insight into the world of web development happened in 2011, through HTML being part of the IT subject in middle school. A spark of passion for web design was ignited in there, but it wasn't until much later that I would decide on a career.

In parallel to high school, I studied mechatronics, a certificate program that combined mechanics, electrics and IT - Where we were introduced to C#, my first programming language. Seeing I enjoyed coding, but not mechanics, I decided to continue my studies in the specific field.

During university I was presented to C, Java and SQL, as well as many concepts linked to behind-the-scenes programming, which I didn't dislike, but proved to be exhausting. In 2019, however, I was assigned to make an app with a small team of students, where I was assigned to front-end development, and I realized that was a possible path to pursue.

After graduating, with renewed interest, I've been dedicating my time to learning how to become a good web developer, while searching for a job.
